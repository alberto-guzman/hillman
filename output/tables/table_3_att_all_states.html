Analytic sample is restricted to HS graduating cohorts 2018–2021. The 2022 and 2023 cohorts are excluded because the NSC query that produced the outcome data has incomplete (2022) or no (2023) enrollment coverage. All panels condition on has_nsc_record = 1 (the student appears in the NSC query).
